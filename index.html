why everyone is doing yoga and why you should too

Yoga has been present for many years. More than 90 percent of people who do yoga focus on flexibility, stress relief and physical fitness. We encourage everyone to participate in yoga.

Yoga can help improve your balance and decrease your cholesterol levels. Studies have shown that yoga reduces the risks of heart disease and help in weight loss. On average, people who practice yoga have lost five pounds.

Another publication used magnetic resonance imagering (MRI) of the brain to show that yoga protects the brain from the decline of gray matter braiin volume as you age. Thus people who practiced yoga have brain volume typical for much younger people!

There are many benefits to spending time in nature.
